About Me

I am a dedicated physician and research leader on a mission to bridge cutting-edge AI innovation with equitable healthcare delivery for the world's most vulnerable. My clinical work is anchored in the complex ecosystem of Turkana—from the remote clinics of Lodwar, to the humanitarian frontlines of the Kakuma Refugee Camp with the IRC, to the integrated community care of Kakuma Mission Hospital.

This on-the-ground experience, uncovering systemic gaps across different models of care, directly fuels my advocacy for scalable, policy-driven solutions and my founding of tech-enabled health initiatives like KAZA.
